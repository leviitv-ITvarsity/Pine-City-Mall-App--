Ocean Basket

Ocean Basket is a South African seafood restaurant chain12. It was first established in Menlyn Park, Pretoria in 1995 by Fats Lazarides, who had a vision of establishing a seafood restaurant that serves delicious dishes from the sea at affordable prices1. The restaurant offers an array of Mediterranean inspired dishes from prawns, sushi, and calamari to their famous fish and chips and their legendary creamy lemon sauce2.
